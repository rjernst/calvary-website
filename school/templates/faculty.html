Christina Grace

Bio

Christina attended Concordia University, Irvine and graduated in 2006 with a degree in liberal studies with a concentration in child development and with a Lutheran Teaching Certification. In January 2008 she completed her credential work and received a teaching certificate from the state of California with CLAD certification. In addition to teaching, Christina runs the Jr. High youth group. In her spare time, Christina likes to spoil her two kittens, play handbells, read, watch old tv shows, play sports, and spend time outdoors.
